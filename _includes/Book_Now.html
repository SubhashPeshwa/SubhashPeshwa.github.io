Booking Process


  1. Enquiry request will be addressed by Alpine Homes Team.
  2. The availability of accommodation along with the tariff details and Alpine Homes Account details will be sent via email.
  3. In order to confirm booking deposit 50% or the total amount into the our account and send us snapshot of the online transfer.
  4. Recieve a confirmation email from Alpine Homes.
  5. Please adhere to these rules to make the process hassle free.
  6. The booking date cannot be postponed if the date of booking is within a week.
  7. The balance amount has to be paid before the check-out.
  8. Accepted Mode of Payments: Internet Banking and Cash Payement.
  9. Credit cards, debit cards and cheques are not accepted.

Cancellation Process


  1. Before 15 days of the check in date --> Full refund.
  2. Within 11 to 15 days of the check in date --> 25% deduction.
  3. Within 6 to 10 days of the check in date --> 40% deduction.
  4. Within 0 to 5 days of the check in date --> No refund.
